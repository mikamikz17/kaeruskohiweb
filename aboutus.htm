    About Kaeru's Kohi

    Kaeru’s Kohi was founded by Mika, a coffee enthusiast and a cozy connoisseur. Mika had this passion in finding unique and splendid taste in coffee, as well as in searching for cafes with comforting ambiance. Cafes to cafes, she struggled to find her ideal one, thus the reason Kaeru’s Kohi was established. Combining her love for making coffee and her passion for coziness, all found here, at her very own coffee shop, Kaeru’s Kohi.
